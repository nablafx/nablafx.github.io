Hello!

My name is Zhang Fu, family name in front. You can call me Jerry. I graduated from King's College London with a first class BEng and a distinction MSc degree. Followed by a software engineer career at Huawei. I am currently employed in the Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a research assistant and I will be starting my PhD here at the PolyU from September 2025.

Vision

Leverage computational advancements to enhance the quality of life and improve healthcare outcomes for individuals facing mental and physical challenges, guiding them toward a positive and vibrant future.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the fields of human-centred computing, robotics and application- and clinical-oriented fundamental computer science research, through which I am dedicated to deliver clinical solutions to realise my vision, the long-standing ambition of mine to make a meaningful impact.

Followings are topics I am paritcularly interested

  • Human-computer interaction (HCI), Robotics and Human-robot Interaction (HRI)
    • for Healthcare Applications
    • for Clinical Applications
    • for Accessiblity
  • HCI and HRI design approches
  • Aritificial Intelligence algorithms and applications
    • Computer visions
    • Generative models
    • Pretrained language models

With a interdisciplinary background in computer science, mathematics, and engineering, I have a strong understanding of the fundamental principles of computing and theories of computation and electronics, along with extensive experience in pratical software development. While my research focuses on the application and delivers software- and hardware-based realiable clicnical solutions, I am also passionate about optimising the fundamental and theoretical algorithms and methods on which deployed application-oriented research is based.
